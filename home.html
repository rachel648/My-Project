History of Le pearl

Since 2010, Le Pearl Restaurant has grown into one of the premier restaurant groups in the Kenyan metropolitan area. Founded by Wendy Gladys and Rachel Murithi, the Pearl Restaurant Group began as a partnership renovating the Imperial Hotel in Kisumu, and transforming it into the Victoria Restaurant. After the transformation of The Imperial Hotel into a successful enterprise Wendy and Rachel felt they could take their talents, expertise and philosophy to other restaurants in the area. Next followed a series of successful restaurants redesigns and launches. In 2015, they opened La Casa Suit and Restaurant in Naivasha, Kenya, Red House in Nakuru, Kenya. Immediately following the opening of Elm Street in 2017, the partners found an incredible space in Nairobi, Kenya, and began construction on Le pearl.
